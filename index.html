Clément Bonet

I am a postdoctoral researcher at ENSAE/CREST, working with Anna Korba on Wasserstein Gradient Flows.

My researchs lie at the interface of Optimal Transport, Computational Statistics and Deep Learning.

I defended my PhD in November 2023 and I was supervised by François Septier, Nicolas Courty and Lucas Drumetz. Before that, I graduated from Telecom Paris and from the MVA ("Mathématiques, Vision, Apprentissage") of Ecole Normale Superieure Paris-Saclay. For more informations, you can find my resume here.
