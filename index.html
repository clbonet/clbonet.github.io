Clément Bonet

I am a PHD student under the supervision of François Septier, Nicolas Courty and Lucas Drumetz since 2020. My researchs lie at the interface of Optimal Transport, Computational Statistics and Deep Learning.

I graduated from Telecom Paris and from the MVA ("Mathématiques, Vision, Apprentissage") of Ecole Normale Superieure Paris-Saclay. For more informations, you can find my resume here.

I am currently looking for a postdoc. Feel free to reach me if you are interested in my research.
